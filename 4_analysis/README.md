## Question to answer
> How has open-source activity around AI/ML repositories changed compared with
> general software-development repositories from 2021 to 2026?

Answer it with: a headline finding, at least one time-series chart (AI vs
general monthly activity), and one comparison chart (growth rate or event-type
mix). Tie any visible jumps to real AI-tool release dates.

## Poster must contain
- Motivating narrative (why this question matters)
- Final analysis results + visualizations
- The three required sections: **Profiling**, **Breaking**, **Scaling**
- A link back to https://github.com/kgr-17/CS131_final_project

## Suggested charts
1. Monthly event volume: AI/ML repos vs general repos (2021–2026).
2. Year-over-year growth rate, AI/ML vs general.
3. Event-type mix (push/PR/issue/star/fork) for AI/ML repos over time.
